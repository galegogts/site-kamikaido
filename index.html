OVERVIEW

When someone dies, they either go to Tengoku (heaven) or Jigoku (hell). If they lived an exceptionally good or an exceptionally evil life, they may go straight to either. However, for most people—who have done both good and evil in their lives—the spirit travels to Reikai (the spiritual world in Japanese mythology) and face many tests by the gods of the dead, walking through the Kami Kaido (The road of gods) , A hero spirit must prove its value purifying the road defeating the evil Yokais (spiritual creatures in Japanese folklore) to finally have an audience with the great King Emma, so they are sent either to Tengoku or Jigoku.
The Reikai is a terrible place—though nowhere near as terrible as Jigoku. It is dark, windy, and full of horrible sights, creatures, sounds, tests and trials. It is a long journey, with no place to rest or find comfort.

KAMI KAIDO is a free-to-play and play to earn collectible NFT card game with hand drawing pixel arts and strategic deck building role-playing game that incorporates elements from many other genres to create a unique retro gaming experience. You will also discover the mysteries of Reikai running expeditions to collect rewards and resources to the Kuraimura village, meet many spirits and create your own paths proving your value defeatIng Yokais, the evil inhabitants of Reikai lurking you beyond the Kami Kaido.

FEATURES


  • Endless Adventure: Select your hero and build your deck before setting out on each expedition along a randomly generated loop path. No expedition is ever the same as the ones before it.
  • Deck Building: Strategically place buildings, landscapes, and yokai cards along each expedition to create your own dangerous path. Find balance between the cards to increase your chances of survival while recovering valuable rewards and resources.
  • Play to Earn: Strike down stronger enemies and claim their loot like NFT items, scrolls and KAMI COINS along the way.
  • Limited NFT Card Collection: Each set collection released has a limited number of NFT pixel art cards with different rarities that you can acquire by buying booster packs.
  • Crafting System: Everytime you complete an expedition along the Kami Kaido you can turn hard-earned resources into your village to upgrade your stations and unlock useful skills to craft NFT Items.


HERO CLASSES - STARTER DECKS



The Ronin

A Ronin is a masterless warrior cast adrift in the world, but still clinging to the remnants of his former life. The taint of their dishonor hangs above them like a dark cloud, using their swordsmanship kiai techniques, throw themselves into combat with reckless abandon.


The Shinobi

A Shinobi move through the shadows, striking down the unwary and vanishing again with ease, blending his training in stealth and assassination with a focused mind. Their rigorous preparation sharpens their minds and bodies.


The Samurai

A Samurai is a loyal warrior that's once in service of a lord. The reputation of samurai for being tenacious in combat often precedes them in battle, and their mere presence is often enough to make dishonorable enemies slink away in the darkness.


The Shinshoku

A Shinshoku is a priest of Shinto religion able to use esoteric powers focusing around combating malevolent forces. Masters of exorcisms, purification, blessing, curses and seals, their practices channeling divine powers of the Kamis.


The Yumihiki

A Yumihiki put their very essence into the martial art of archery. Every part of the movement demanded total commitment, complete devotion of the self. If their heart was not in your arrow, it would never find your enemy's heart.

GAME MODES

Battle Mode

Play against other players in Kami Kaido and defeat them to looting all that they earn in every match. Climb in a ranking system to claim great rewards after the end of each season.

Expedition Mode

Select your hero class and deck before setting out on each expedition along a randomly generated loop path. No expedition is ever the same as the ones before it.

Story Mode

Each chapter is a complete new gaming experience. Invite your friends to a unique CO-OP mode system and defeat together the most powerfull Yokais in Kami Kaido.
